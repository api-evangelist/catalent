Elpida Therapeutics, a non-profit biotechnology company developing gene therapies for ultra-rare diseases, and Catalent, Inc., the leader in enabling the development and supply of better treatments for patients worldwide, today announced a strategic partnership to support late-phase manufacturing of Elpida’s lead program, an AAV9 gene therapy for Spastic Paraplegia Type 50 (SPG50).
